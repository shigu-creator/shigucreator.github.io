My Works

主要角色：奥托·索菲斯（Otto Sophis） - 机械师

角色背景：曾是瓦尔希亚最顶尖的机械师，擅长构造和修复精密的机械装置。在故事的开端，奥托与人机混合物保持着表面上的互利关系，利用彼此的资源维持生计。然而，在一场阴谋中，人机混合物背叛了奥托，并摧毁了他的身体，仅剩下他的一只机械手——这只手与他的创造物、穿孔玩偶伊尔玛合为一体。现在，他满怀复仇的渴望，决心推翻统治瓦尔希亚的腐败力量。

Main Character: Otto Sophis - Mechanic

Character Background: Once the top mechanic in Valshia, Otto excelled in constructing and repairing precision machinery. At the story's beginning, Otto maintains a superficial symbiotic relationship with human-machine hybrids, leveraging each other’s resources to survive. However, he is betrayed by the hybrids in a conspiracy that destroys his body, leaving only a mechanical arm—a fusion of his creation, the perforated doll Irma. Now, driven by vengeance, he is determined to overthrow the corrupt powers ruling Valshia.

Visual Artworks

视觉作品说明

本作品以电影《遁入虚无》为灵感源泉，通过个人化的艺术表达，呈现出一种更为深邃的宇宙观：万物皆由一种伟大而隐秘的灵性力量所孕育和主导。这种灵性存在贯穿于自然的每一个角落，掌控着世间的平衡。作品不仅致力于探索自然界万物的灵性，更关注个体与自然之间的微妙联系，唤醒观者对生命本质的思考。通过融合流动的光影、象征性意象和多层次的视觉表现，作品试图传达一种对宇宙的敬畏之心——人在自然的面前并非主宰，而是共生的伙伴，彼此之间存在着深刻而不可分割的纽带。

Visual Artwork Description

Inspired by Enter the Void, this artwork channels a unique vision that conveys a profound philosophy: all beings are born from and guided by an immense, hidden spiritual force that weaves through every element of nature, balancing the fabric of existence. The work does not merely delve into the spirituality of the natural world but also highlights the intricate relationship between the individual and nature, inviting viewers to contemplate the essence of life itself. Through fluid light, symbolic imagery, and layered visual narratives, the piece seeks to evoke a reverence for the universe—a recognition that humanity is not the master of nature but a symbiotic partner, bound by an unbreakable, profound connection.

视觉作品说明

本作品从电影《龙纹身的女孩》中汲取灵感，并融入了个人化的艺术视角，探索亚文化与个性表达的深层意义。作品通过冷峻的视觉语言和张扬的艺术风格，呈现出一种不拘一格的自我表达，彰显出一种超越主流文化的个性力量。在这种表达中，亚文化不仅仅是标新立异的象征，更是对传统规则的挑战和对个人真实自我的探寻。作品试图引导观者重新审视个性与自我的本质，揭示亚文化之中的复杂情感和思想，反映出一种对自由、真实和独特身份的执着追求。

Visual Artwork Description

Inspired by The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo, this artwork incorporates a personal artistic perspective to delve into the themes of subculture and individual expression. Through stark visual elements and bold stylistic choices, the piece portrays an unrestrained mode of self-expression, highlighting a force of individuality that transcends mainstream norms. Here, subculture is not merely a symbol of rebellion but a medium for challenging conventional rules and uncovering an authentic self. The work seeks to invite viewers to re-examine the essence of identity and individuality, revealing the complex emotions and philosophies within subculture—a relentless pursuit of freedom, authenticity, and unique identity.

视觉作品说明

本作品以秘鲁亚马逊地区的阿沙宁卡人文化和阿雅瓦斯卡宗教仪式为灵感，将个人创意融入其中，以一种独特的方式诠释了原住民对于自然与灵性世界的敬畏与探索。作品以沉浸式的视觉效果，呈现出阿雅瓦斯卡仪式中的神秘氛围，彰显了亚马逊原住民独特的信仰与精神追求。通过流动的色彩、象征性图腾和象征着灵性觉醒的视觉元素，作品试图展现一种超越物质的存在感，强调个体在自然中的微妙位置。作品邀请观者反思现代文明与自然信仰之间的张力，唤起对个性与精神自由的深层追求，表达了一种对文化根源和灵性探索的真挚敬意。

Visual Artwork Description

This artwork draws inspiration from the Ashaninka people of the Peruvian Amazon and the Ayahuasca religious ceremony, incorporating personal creative elements to portray a unique vision of reverence for the natural and spiritual realms. With immersive visual effects, the piece seeks to evoke the mystical atmosphere of the Ayahuasca ritual, celebrating the Ashaninka’s distinctive spirituality and their pursuit of transcendence. Through flowing colors, symbolic totems, and elements that signify spiritual awakening, the work highlights an existence that transcends the material world, emphasizing the individual's subtle role within nature. This piece invites viewers to reflect on the tension between modern civilization and indigenous spiritual beliefs, awakening a deep-rooted yearning for individuality and spiritual freedom. It serves as an homage to cultural heritage and the journey of spiritual exploration.
